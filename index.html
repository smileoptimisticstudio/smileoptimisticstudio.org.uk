Smile Optimistic Studio CIC · Manchester, UK

Made for the long hours in hospital.

Smile Optimistic Studio publishes the Hospital Joy Toolkit — a set of Emotional Support Cards for children in long-term medical treatment. The first set, twenty-five cards for ages 4 to 6, is in print. Two further sets, for ages 7 to 10 and 11 to 15, are in development.

Who this is for

Paediatric wards. Children's charities.
Hospices and long-stay units.

On the ward

The cards arrive packaged for a ward, with a short letter for staff. They are designed to be picked up, read aloud, and returned to — not stored on a shelf.

In the room

Each card is read aloud by a parent, nurse, or play specialist with the child. The work is artistic, not clinical. It does not replace psychological support, and it is not asked to.

Where we work

NHS and private paediatric wards across the United Kingdom. Charities supporting children with serious illness. Hospices providing long-term and end-of-life care.

A note from the studio · 17 April 2026

The studio was started while my daughter Lada was in long-term oncology treatment in Manchester. Two diagnoses, two rounds of treatment, and six years lived in and out of hospital — the kind of returning obstacles anyone who has been on this ward will recognise. Lada has given her permission for her name and her story to be part of the studio's record; the work is dedicated to her, and to every child whose hours look like hers.

What I needed during those hours — and what other parents and nurses needed — were small things that did not pretend to be therapy, and did not pretend to be nothing. Things to read aloud. Things to draw on. Things to keep.

Smile Optimistic Studio publishes those small things. The first set — twenty-five cards for children aged 4 to 6, the Hospital Joy Toolkit — is in print and reaching the first hospitals. Two more sets follow, for older children and for the long teenage hours which are different again.

The studio works with paediatric wards, children's charities, and family support organisations in the United Kingdom. If your institution would like to receive the first set, or hold one for review, please write to me directly.

Yuliia Barsukova Founder & Designated Safeguarding Lead · Smile Optimistic Studio CIC
